About Us

Kintosh Laundry offers professional laundry and dry-cleaning services with same-day pickup and delivery. We’re committed to giving your clothes the best care.

Our Services

🧺 Wash & Fold

Our Wash & Fold service is ideal for everyday wear. We wash, dry, and neatly fold your laundry for easy storage.

We handle:
  • T-shirts
  • Trousers & Jeans
  • Underwear
  • Light bedsheets
  • Socks & Towels

👕 Ironing & Pressing

We use safe heat and steam ironing techniques to keep your clothes wrinkle-free and sharp-looking.

Items typically pressed:
  • Shirts & Blouses
  • Trousers & Skirts
  • Uniforms
  • School Clothes
  • Office Attire

🧥 Dry Cleaning

Perfect for delicate or expensive clothing that can’t be machine-washed. We use non-water-based solvents to clean your items gently.

Dry cleaned items include:
  • Suits & Blazers
  • Dresses & Gowns
  • Winter Jackets
  • Delicate Fabrics (e.g., Silk, Velvet)

🛏️ Duvet Cleaning

We deep-clean your duvets and bed covers using industrial-grade washers. Our process removes dust mites, odors, and bacteria.

Duvet types cleaned:
  • Small and Large Duvets
  • Comforters
  • Mattress Covers
  • Thick Bedspreads

🧽 Carpet Cleaning

Our carpet cleaning removes stains, dirt, and allergens. We use deep shampooing and fast-dry techniques to bring carpets back to life.

We clean:
  • Fluffy Carpets
  • Wool Rugs
  • Synthetic Mats
  • Bathroom and Entry Rugs
